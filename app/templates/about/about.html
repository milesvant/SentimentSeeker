Tech Stack

A lot of different libraries and frameworks went into the making of this website. At the base level, the backend is made with the Flask web framework. I've used many helpful Flask extensions such as Flask-SQLAlchemy and Flask-Bootstrap to integrate the other technologies I used into the backend. My ORM of choice is SQLAlchemy, and the current database used is SQLite3. I use Redis Queue as my job queue, and TextBlob to perform natural language processing tasks. On the frontend, I use Bootstrap 3 as well as jQuery.
